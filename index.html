About Me

I come from a background in Electronics Engineering and hold a Master’s degree in Business Administration from The University of Northampton, UK with a thesis on Project Management. My admiration for coding began during my tenure as a database administrator for one of the leading software services company and continued to grow as I worked as an intern post MBA, where I was responsible for maintaining and making improvements for the company’s website.

I realized over this course how much I love solving problems and the sheer joy I derived from creating new things. The program also gave me opportunities to participate in hackathons, individual and group projects. I am now looking forward for opportunities to further fuel my passion for coding.

In my personal life I enjoy sketching and love travelling and exploring new places.
